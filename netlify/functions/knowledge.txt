## Covenant Epistemology Notes
Covenant epistemology (Michael Polanyi, Esther Lightcap Meek) holds that knowing is a covenantal act —
the knower pledges fiduciary commitment to a reality that is not fully reducible to explicit propositions.
Contact with reality is personal, responsible, and transformative.
